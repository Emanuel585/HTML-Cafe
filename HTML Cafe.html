Classic Lasagna Recipe

Servings: 6-8

Prep Time: 30-40 min

Cook Time: 45 mins

Total Time: About 1 and a 1/2 hours

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound (450 g) ground beef (or half beef, half Italian sausage)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz / 800 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz / 425 g) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar (optional, balances acidity)
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

For the Ricotta mix:

  • 15 oz (425 g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tsp dried)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Other layers:

  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles (if not oven-ready)

    2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook noodles according to package directions, then drain and lay flat on parchment paper to prevent sticking.

  2. Make the meat sauce
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add onion and cook until soft (about 5 minutes).
    • Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
    • Add ground beef and cook until browned; drain excess fat.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, sugar, basil, oregano, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
    • Simmer uncovered for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Prepare the ricotta mix

    4. In a medium bowl, mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper until smooth.

  4. Assemble the lasagna

    5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

    In a 9x13-inch baking dish:

    • Spread 1 cup of sauce on the bottom.
    • Add a layer of noodles.
    • Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture.
    • Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella.
    • Repeat layers (sauce → noodles → ricotta → mozzarella) two more times.
    • Finish with sauce and remaining mozzarella + Parmesan on top.
  5. Bake
    • Cover with foil (to prevent sticking, spray the underside with cooking spray).
    • Bake 25 minutes covered, then remove foil and bake another 20–25 minutes until bubbling and golden.
    • Let rest 10–15 minutes before slicing (important for clean layers).
  6. Enjoy

    7. Now you can finally grab a slice of your delicious Lasagna!

About lasagna

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish that has earned its place as a comfort food classic all around the world. Originating from the Emilia-Romagna region of Italy, traditional lasagna features layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta or béchamel, and a generous topping of melted cheese.

What makes lasagna special is its perfect balance of textures and flavors — soft noodles, savory sauce, and gooey cheese all baked together into one delicious bite. Every layer tells a story of Italian tradition, love for food, and the joy of sharing a meal with others.
